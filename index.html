The studio

Hi, I'm Priyanka —
I photograph real moments, not poses.

Behind every good photograph is someone who noticed the small thing — the hand you didn't know you were holding, the laugh mid-sentence, the light at exactly 5:47pm. That's what I chase, camera in hand, across weddings, homes, studios and streets.

Whatever the occasion — a wedding, a nursery not yet finished, a birthday candle about to go out — I show up, I watch closely, and I hand you back a story you'll want to keep looking at.
